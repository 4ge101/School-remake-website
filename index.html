About Our School

Satyanarayan secondary school is one of the oldest historical institution. Currently, this school is conducting classes from six to twelve. The school team under visionary principalship is trying to impart scientific, practical, and job-oriented education to all.

The school is conducting classes from 4 to 12 in Nepali medium from 2009, in English medium from 2072, and electrical engineering from class 9 to 12 under technical and vocational stream from 2073. The total number of students is approximately 1980 i.e. 400 students in Nepali medium, 900 in English medium, and 600 In grades 11 and 12. The computer is taught as a subject from class 4 to 12 and in Electrical engineering.
