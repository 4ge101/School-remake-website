ABOUT US

Satynarayan Secondary School

Satyanarayan Secondary school is one of the oldest and historical institution. It was at first incepted by the Marwari Community family to educate their offspring. Currently, this school runs classes from four to twelve. The school team under visionary Principalship to impact scientific, practical, and job-oriented education for all. This school runs classes in Nepali medium from 2009, in English medium from 2072, and electrical engineering from class 9 to 12 under technical and vocational stream from 2074.
